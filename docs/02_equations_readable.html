Governing Equations (Readable Version)

From docs/02_physics_math.md and docs/02a_governing_equation_derivation.md

Assumptions (v1)

  • Two immiscible phases: water and oil
  • Incompressible fluids and rock
  • No capillary pressure, no gravity
  • Constant porosity

1) Phase Conservation

For each phase α in {w, o}:

φ ∂Sα ∂t + ∇⋅uα =qα
Sw+So=1

2) Darcy Law and Mobilities

uα = - λα K ∇p
λα = krαμα
λt=λw+λo , ut=uw+uo =-λtK∇p

3) Pressure Equation (Elliptic)

∇⋅ut=qt , qt=qw+qo
∇⋅(-λtK∇p)=qt

4) Water Transport Equation

fw= λwλt , uw=fwut
φ ∂Sw ∂t +∇⋅(fwut) =qw

Final v1 Coupled System

∇⋅(-λtK∇p)=qt φ ∂Sw ∂t +∇⋅(fwut)=qw ut=-λtK∇p , fw=λwλt
